Aarsha Bharatha Vidyalaya was started in May 2002 to fulfill a pressing need for a modern play- way method of imparting education to pre-school children. The manager and staff of Chempaka Kindergarten, a reputed and established pre- primary school at Thiruvananthapuram are involved in the setting up of the school. An effective and friendly system has been adopted at Aarsha Bharatha, the aims of which are to:

  • Form sound educational basis
  • Encourage originality and initiative in children
  • Develop their latent talents
  • Help them to be self reliant
  • Expose them to modern methods of education
  • Impart sound moral values
  • Make them eco-conscious
  • Make learning a joyous adventure
  • Small class norm for individual attention

Curriculum

The curriculum takes care of the all-round development of the child. The School Day will be held every year when all the children will be given a chance to exhibit their talents on stage.

Other information

  • The School is very keen on fostering a healthy parent-teacher relationship.
  • There will be an Open Day at the end of each term when the parents themselves must meet the Class Teacher and discuss their child's progress.

Admission

The age of admission to the playgroup and LKG are 2½ + and 3½ + respectively . Any physical disability or allergy should be disclosed at the time of admission.

Fee

The admission fee is payable once at the time of admission. The tuition fee is payable in three equal installments at the beginning of each term. The tuition fee for the term is to be paid within 10 days of the commencement of a term. Thereafter, a fine of Rs. 2/- per day will have to be paid. The admission and tuition fee are subject to revision yearly. A fee, once paid, is not refundable and a receipt will we issued for the same.

Terms, Holidays and Punctuality.

The academic year is from June to March. Individual attention is possible as the maximum number admitted to a class will be 20-25.

The academic year will have three terms. Details of terms and holidays will be intimated to parents on the School Notice Board and through the School Diary which the parents must go through everyday.

The diary has to be signed by one of the parents everyday and sent with the child to School. Classes will begin at 9:45 a.m. and end at 3:00 p.m. Monday to Friday. Play Group children may be taken home at 12:30 p.m.

Parents are specially requested to be punctual in bringing their children to school and in taking them back at 3:00 p.m. Any person other than parents coming to collect the child from school should be personally introduced to the Class Teacher or Principal.
